# About the Critical Thinking Series

## Mission

The Critical Thinking Series aims to equip participants with practical skills for better reasoning, decision-making, and problem-solving in both personal and professional contexts.

## Presenter

**Pietro Griffa** brings together experience in education and practical applications of critical thinking principles. The series emphasizes hands-on learning and real-world application.

## Learning Approach

Our teaching philosophy centers on:

- **Interactive Learning**: Engaging presentations with practical exercises
- **Real-World Application**: Examples and scenarios from everyday life
- **Progressive Building**: Each session builds on previous concepts
- **Open Resources**: All materials freely available for continued learning

## Target Audience

This series is designed for:

- Professionals seeking to improve decision-making skills
- Students interested in logical reasoning
- Anyone curious about how we think and make decisions
- Organizations looking to enhance team reasoning capabilities

## Format and Schedule

- **Duration**: 60-75 minutes per session
- **Frequency**: Weekly sessions
- **Materials**: Slides, handouts, interactive demos, and references provided
- **Follow-up**: Take-home materials for continued practice
