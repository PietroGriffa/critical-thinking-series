# Critical Thinking Series

Welcome to the Critical Thinking Series documentation! This series is designed to help participants develop better reasoning skills, recognize cognitive biases, avoid logical fallacies, and make more informed decisions.

## What You'll Learn

Throughout this series, you'll gain practical tools for:

- **Better Decision Making**: Learn to evaluate options more systematically
- **Bias Recognition**: Identify when your thinking might be influenced by cognitive shortcuts
- **Argument Analysis**: Distinguish between strong and weak reasoning
- **Problem Solving**: Apply structured approaches to complex challenges
