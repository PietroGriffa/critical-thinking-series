# Topics Overview

The Critical Thinking Series covers fundamental concepts and practical applications of clear reasoning. Each topic builds upon previous concepts while introducing new tools and techniques.

## Series Structure

Each topic follows a consistent format:

- **Learning Objectives**: Clear goals for what you'll gain
- **Interactive Presentation**: Engaging slides with examples
- **Practical Exercises**: Hands-on application of concepts
- **Take-Home Materials**: References and practice exercises
- **Demo Activities**: Interactive demonstrations of key principles

## Topic Progression

The series is designed to build knowledge progressively:

1. **Foundation** - Core concepts and importance of critical thinking
2. **Psychology** - Understanding how our minds work and where they can lead us astray
3. **Logic** - Formal and informal reasoning principles
4. **Application** - Putting it all together in real-world scenarios
