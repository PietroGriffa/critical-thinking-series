# Demo: Identifying Assumptions

This demo helps participants practice identifying hidden assumptions in everyday statements.

## Exercise 1: Restaurant Reviews

Read the following restaurant review and identify the assumptions:

> "This restaurant is terrible. We waited 20 minutes for a table even though we had a reservation. The service was slow, and the food was cold. I can't believe they charge $30 for pasta!"

**Possible Assumptions:**
- A 20-minute wait is always unacceptable
- Having a reservation should guarantee immediate seating
- The reviewer's experience is representative of all experiences
- $30 is inherently too much for pasta, regardless of quality or portion

## Exercise 2: News Headlines

Analyze these headlines for assumptions:

1. "Local Business Thrives Despite Economic Downturn"
2. "New Study Proves Exercise Prevents Disease"
3. "Technology Making Kids Less Social"

## Discussion Questions

1. How can we check our assumptions?
2. What questions should we ask before accepting a claim?
3. When might assumptions be reasonable vs. problematic?
