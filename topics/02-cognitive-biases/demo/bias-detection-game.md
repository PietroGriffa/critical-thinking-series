# Demo: Bias Detection Game

Interactive exercises to help identify cognitive biases in realistic scenarios.

## Scenario 1: Investment Decision

**Situation**: Sarah has been investing in Company X stock for 5 years. The stock has consistently declined, but she keeps buying more shares because she's already invested so much money.

**Question**: What bias might Sarah be experiencing?

**Answer**: Sunk Cost Fallacy - continuing to invest because of past investments rather than future prospects.

## Scenario 2: Job Interview

**Situation**: A hiring manager meets a candidate who reminds him of his successful colleague from his previous company. He immediately has a positive impression and asks easier questions than usual.

**Question**: What bias is occurring?

**Answer**: Halo Effect / Similarity Bias - positive impression influencing overall judgment.

## Scenario 3: Medical Diagnosis

**Situation**: Dr. Smith recently saw three patients with a rare condition. When the next patient comes in with similar symptoms, he immediately suspects the same rare condition, even though common conditions are much more likely.

**Question**: What bias might be affecting Dr. Smith?

**Answer**: Availability Heuristic - recent examples making something seem more probable than it actually is.
